Rating Components

For Hotels & Restaurants

  • Food Quality

    Freshness and condition of donated food

  • Punctuality

    Timeliness of food preparation and handover

  • Communication

    Responsiveness and clarity in interactions

For NGOs & Collectors

  • Reliability

    Consistency in collection and distribution

  • Professionalism

    Conduct and efficiency during collections

  • Impact

    Effectiveness in food distribution to those in need
